## Video Format

Default specs:

- 9:16 vertical
- 1080 x 1920
- 12-25 seconds
- hook in first 1.5 seconds
- large captions in safe center area
- no text near bottom UI/caption zone
- always include CTA in caption and final 2 seconds

## Creative Pillars

1. **Not A Robot** - compare bad bot reply vs better human reply.
2. **Night Thought** - "I wrote one sentence at 2 AM".
3. **Memory** - yesterday/today continuation.
4. **Decision Help** - chaos to next step.
5. **Focus** - quick reset for work.
6. **Premium Depth** - free vs Pro/Premium difference without hard selling.

## Production Workflow

For every video:

1. Record or mock chat screen.
2. Add title hook in first second.
3. Add voiceover or captions.
4. Keep all public copy safe and non-explicit.
5. Use source/campaign start link:
   - TikTok organic: `src_tiktok__cmp_org_video_01`
   - Reels organic: `src_instagram__cmp_reels_video_01`
   - YouTube Shorts: `src_youtube__cmp_shorts_video_01`
6. Pin comment with profile link CTA.
7. Log views, profile clicks, bot starts, first messages, 3+ messages, paywall views.

## Moderation Rules

Avoid:

- explicit adult content
- dating/casual sex positioning
- drug mentions
- claims that the bot replaces therapy, doctors, friends, or relationships
- "you are lonely" targeting
- minors or youth-coded visuals

Use:

- AI-компаньон
- живой диалог
- память контекста
- приватный разговор
- поддержка, разбор, фокус
- бесплатно попробовать
